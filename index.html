HOW TO SURVIVE TILLERY

MRS. TILLERY ALWAYS STARTS AT CAM 11. USE THE AUDIO LURE TO KEEP HER AWAY FROM YOU.

MAKE SURE YOU'RE PLAYING THE SOUND NEXT TO THE CAMERA WHERE SHE IS. IF SHE GETS CLOSE CLICK E TO CLOSE THE DOOR.

USING THE AUDIO LURE TOO MUCH WILL BREAK THE CAMERAS. USING THE POWERED DOOR WILL CAUSE IT TO BREAK. FIX THEM AT THE CONTROL PANEL.
